Pork Masala Pasta - 5/28/19, 8:44 PM

Tim and I cooked masala pasta with pork. He bought Garam Masala powder from Walmart and we added it along with a bunch of other spices. I cooked the pork with garlic and jalapenos to add flavor. At the same time, Tim put the pasta in the pot of boiling water. We then took the meat out and cooked the squash and eggplant with the spices since they take a while. After that cooked for a little, we added the rest of the vegetables. Next, we added back the meat and cooked for a little longer. At last, we added the pasta and cooked everything together.
